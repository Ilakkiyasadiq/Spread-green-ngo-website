Protected Private Forests



We, in Spread Green plant over 100 000 saplings every year in our protected environment and take good care of
them and nurture them so that we can increase the green of cover of the area , therefore directly impacting
on the global environmental change. We hope this is a small but integral part of humanity's efforts against
climate change. We have this 'Protected Private Forests' around the world each equiped with a well trained and
knowledgable team who are passionate about this service, we looking forward expand it to even more countries in near future.
